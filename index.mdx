## Quick Start

Choose your emulator based on the games you want to play:

- **Project64 MPN**: For Mario Party 1, 2, and 3 (N64 games)
- **Dolphin MPN**: For Mario Party 4, 5, 6, 7, and 8 (GameCube games)
